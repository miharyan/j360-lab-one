Timeline of Soekarno's Life

1901-1970

21 June 1970 Died at Wisma Yaso, Jakarta
11 March 1966 Signed the Supersemar controversial warrant
18 August 1945 Became the first president of Indonesia. He served two terms of presidency until 21 March 1967
17 August 1945 Soekarno along with Mohammad Hatta proclaimed the independence of Indonesia
1 June 1945 Pancasila, the five pillars of Indonesia's state ideology, was introduced by Soekarno
1942 Free return during the Japanese occupation
1933 Arrested and exiled to the province of New Bengkulu
1932 Joined the Party of Indonesia (Partindo)
1929 Arrested by Netherlands because of Sukarno's PNI (Nationalist Party Indonesia)
1926 Founded Algemene Study Club in Bandung
1925 Graduated from Technische Hoge School (ITB) in Bandung
1920 Graduated from HBS
1915 Schooled to Hoogere Burger School (HBS)
6 June 1901 Born at Surabaja (now called Surabaya)), Java, Dutch East Indies
